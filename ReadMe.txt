german_data.csv
refers to the Statlog (German Credit Data) Data Set from UCI Machine Learning Repository.
Full description can be found here: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Statlog+(German+Credit+Data)

Attribute Information:
	Attribute name	Description
1	Sta	Status of existing checking account
2	Dur	Duration in month
3	CreH	Credit history
4	Pur	Purpose
5	CreA	Credit amount
6	Sav	Savings account/bonds
7	PreE	Present employment since
8	Ins	Installment rate in percentage of disposable income
9	Per	Personal status and sex
10	OthD	Other debtors / guarantors
11	PreR	Present residence since
12	Pro	Property
13	Age	Age in years
14	OthI	Other installment plans
15	Hou	Housing
16	NumE	Number of existing credits at this bank
17	Job	Job
18	NumP	Number of people being liable to provide maintenance for
19	Tel	Telephone
20	for	foreign worker
